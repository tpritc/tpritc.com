Podcast Chapters

2015–2016


Podcast Chapters is a Mac Application designed and developed to make adding chapter markers to podcasts as simple and quick as possible. Prior to Podcast Chapters, users had to upload their podcast file to a web application such as Auphonic, add the chapters, and then download it. Podcast Chapters is a native Mac application designed to be instantly recognisable to anyone who uses a Mac day-to-day, with clear iconography, a clean asthetic, and platform-standard keyboard shortcuts.


Notes

This was an independent, personal design and development project I worked on in my spare time. It was not for any company or client.

All of the digital design work for Podcast Chapters was made in Sketch, the great interface design software from Bohemian Coding. It was written in Swift.

You can visit the marketing site for Podcast Chapters here. Podcast Chapters is now available in the Mac App Store for $19.99.
